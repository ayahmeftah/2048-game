How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) to slide the tiles.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one.
  • If all cells are filled with tiles and no merge is possible, you lose.
  • Reach 2048 to win. Good luck! ⭐
